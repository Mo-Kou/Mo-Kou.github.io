Some ways to help the spiritual community are by:

Sharing the Gospel Praying Giving additional Support (Optional)
Talk with your friends about God Pray that everybody around the world will all eventually be able to know Christ in their own way Help the people in poverty by giving money, food, and hope to all who have lost it
Share with your family members (If they don't know Christ yet) Pray for the physically challenged, and that they will renew their relationship with God Recommend the bible to all sorts of people you know
Study the gospel, so that when you are asked the question: "who and what is Jesus?", you will be able to answer, and help them into true christianity. Pray for yourself, so that you will overcome any challenges in your life Invite people to go to church and worship together with you
Create unique ways to share God's good news with the WORLD!!! Pray for your friends and family that they will repent and turn towards God whenever they have sinned Treat others the way you want to be treated, and be kind to EVERYONE whether you like them or not.
